Hello! I’m P M Abrar Ishraq Toha. I am from Bangladesh. I am 18 years old and currently a student in 12th grade.

I studied at Khalgram High School and now I’m continuing my education at Varendra College Rajshahi.

I’ve loved technology since I was a child. Around the age of 15 or 16, I taught myself electrical wiring. Later, I started fixing small hardware problems on my own.

Over time, I learned programming and developed a few Android apps. I also built some websites and explored robotics and robot development. I enjoy working with both software and hardware, and I love creating solutions that combine the two.
